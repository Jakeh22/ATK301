Our Artists

Jacob Hoffman

Although Jacob is the CEO of the record label, he is also a producer and a DJ. Most of the artists that we work with or sign will work with him in the studio. Jacob is fluent in Ableton Live 10, and knows how to produce, mix and master. He's been making music for 4 years and only plans to get better.

Jay Nebo

Jay was one of the first artists our CEO, Jacob Hoffman met at ISU. Jay is from Deerfield, IL and he is a rapper, singer and songwriter. His voice was definitely something that caught our ears. He started working with the label in 2018 and signed in 2019. He will definitely be a great artist in the near future.

j.p.z

j.p.z is a producer and DJ from Lisle, IL. He produces EDM, specifically future bass and deep house. His first project will be released soon. you can keep up to date with news regarding his music if you follow us on Instagram: @basementbarsLLC

MORE COMING SOON

Over the summer, our label will be focused on signing Chicago-based artists and female singers. Our CEO wants to work with many artists that are from the city. We will be releasing our project soon so we will definitely need a couple more artists. Especially someone who can sing.
